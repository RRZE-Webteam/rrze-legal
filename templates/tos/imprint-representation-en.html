Legal form and representation

The Friedrich-Alexander-Universität Erlangen-Nürnberg (FAU) is a corporation under public law and, at the same time, a state institution pursuant to art. 1 (1) BayHSchG (Bavarian Higher Education Act). The legal representative of the FAU is its President.
