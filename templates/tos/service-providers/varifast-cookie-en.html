Advertising of the company VariFast GmbH

Description and scope of data processing

On some websites of the university are in collaboration with the company VariFast GmbH and switched using the ad server of Adition AG banner ads.

Cookies are also used. These are only session cookies and temporary cookies that serve to statistically measure the number of readers / visitors and limit the number of delivery of advertising material. It will only technical, but no identifying data recorded. All data is only anonymized and evaluated purely statistically.

All relevant data protection regulations, in particular those of the German Telemediengesetz and the Federal Data Protection Act, are provided by the VariFast GmbH and Adition AG complies with the universitys contractual obligations.

Withdrawal

By clicking on the following link, the collection of anonymized Data stopped by our advertising partners. Adition replaces the case in this case current cookie through a new opt-out cookie. This opt-out cookie deletes the so far stored information and prevents further detection of anonymized Information. If this OptOut cookie is deleted, Adition can no longer detect that an opt-out has taken place. In this case repeat the OptOut process.
