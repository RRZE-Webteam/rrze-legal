Tracking pixel of the collecting society Wort

On the website, "session cookies" and JavaScript from VG Wort, Munich, used to measure access to texts, to capture the probability of copying.

Session cookies are small pieces of information that a provider stores in memory of the visitor's computer. A session cookie is a randomly generated unique identification number is stored, a so-called session ID. Also contains a cookie provides information about its origin and the storage period. Session cookies can save no other data. These measurements will be carried out by INFOnline GmbH after Scalable central measuring method (SZM) carried out.

They help to determine the probability of copying individual texts for remuneration to determine the legal claims of authors and publishers. Personal Data are about this Co

Scalable central measurement method (SZM)

The website uses the "Scalable Central Measurement Method" (SZM) of INFOnline GmbH (https://www.infonline.de) for determining statistical parameters to determine the copy probability of texts.

Anonymous measurements are collected. The access number measurement uses for Recognition of computer systems alternatively a session cookie or a signature, created from various automatically transmitted information from your browser will. IP addresses are only processed in an anonymous form.

The process was developed with data protection in mind. Sole goal of The procedure is to determine the copy probability of individual texts.

At no time individual users are identified. Your identity always remains protected. You will not receive any advertising through the system.
